# Highland City Council Work Session Notes (2025-02-10)

Session start: 4:05 PM

## Work Session Topics

1. Capital project sequencing for FY2026 utility replacements.
2. Water-right transfer options for the north pressure zone.
3. Draft procurement strategy for fleet electrification.

## Discussion Highlights

Public Works reported that replacing 2.4 miles of failing line in 2026 would reduce annual emergency repairs by 31 percent.
Finance staff identified a phased grant draw schedule with milestone dates on March 15, June 30, and October 1.
Council requested that bid packages separate trench restoration from material procurement.

## Decisions

- Approved initiation of design services for Segment A utility replacements.
- Authorized staff to publish the request for qualifications by February 21.

## Assigned Actions

- Public Works will submit final scope language for RFQ Exhibit 2 by February 14.
- Finance will provide debt service scenarios for 5-year and 10-year terms.
- City Manager will schedule a joint session with the water district in March.

## Closeout

No vote was taken on funding authorization at this session; final funding action remains on the regular meeting agenda.
