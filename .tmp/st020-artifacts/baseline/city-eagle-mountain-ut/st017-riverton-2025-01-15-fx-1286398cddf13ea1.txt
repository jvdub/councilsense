# Riverton City Council Public Hearing Minutes (2025-01-15)

## Roll Call

Mayor Olsen called the meeting to order at 6:00 PM. Councilmembers present: Carter, Diaz, and Morgan.

## Agenda Item 4 — Public Hearing: R-12 Zoning Amendment

Planning staff presented a proposal for 412 units across 96 acres with a phased buildout timeline ending in 2029.
The hearing included testimony from 17 residents. Several comments requested lower building height near the elementary school.

### Staff Recommendations

- Approve the zoning amendment with a 25-foot height transition requirement.
- Require two additional pedestrian crossings before phase-two permits.
- Publish a traffic impact addendum before final plat approval.

### Council Deliberation and Motions

Councilmember Diaz moved to approve the amendment with the 25-foot transition and crossing requirements.
Councilmember Morgan seconded the motion. The motion passed 3-1 after discussion of school traffic and emergency access.

## Follow-Up Actions

The council directed planning staff to return on February 12 with revised cross-section diagrams.
The city engineer was assigned to publish an updated signal timing memo within 14 days.
