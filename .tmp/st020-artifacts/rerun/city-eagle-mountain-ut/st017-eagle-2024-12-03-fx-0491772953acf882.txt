# City Council Meeting Baseline Review (2024-12-03)

Source: "City Council Meeting 2024-12-03 Minutes Approved Minutes"  
Status: processed  
Confidence: high

## Summary

The council reviewed major residential development proposals totaling 893 units across 208 acres, including open space and HOA park considerations. It approved multiple administrative and infrastructure-related items, including a right-of-way acquisition agreement and a property title transfer tied to the new school district interlocal boundary. The council also approved an updated 2025 meeting schedule with additional meeting dates.

## Decisions and actions

### Key decisions

- Approved a Purchase Agreement with Ivory Land Corporation for acquisition of right-of-way for Old Airport Road, North.
- Consented to transfer of title for property located in the interlocal boundary of the new school district in West Utah County.
- Approved the 2025 City Council Meeting Schedule, including added dates.

### Key actions

- Removed one item from the Consent Agenda and moved it to a scheduled item for further discussion.
- Adopted schedule changes adding meetings on November 5 and December 16.
- Continued discussion/information item participation with planning/development presenters.

## Notable Topics

- Residential growth and land use scale (893 units, 208 acres)
- Open space requirements and HOA parks
- Right-of-way acquisition (Old Airport Road, North)
- Interlocal/school district boundary property transfer
- Governance and calendar planning for 2025
