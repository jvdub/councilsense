1. ORDINANCE / PUBLIC HEARING - CITY CODE AMENDMENT (RESIDENTIAL) FOR SUNSET FLATS

The City Council held a public hearing on Ordinance No. 1234 to amend the City Code and zoning code for residential districts affecting Sunset Flats.
Staff discussed the proposed ordinance amendment, including changes to Chapter 10 and Section 10-5.

A new laundromat is proposed near Sunset Flats and staff recommend approval of the conditional use permit application.

2. ADJOURNMENT
The meeting was adjourned.
